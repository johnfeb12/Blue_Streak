Company History



Thank you for visiting Bluestreak the screen-printing and embroidery specialist for all your custom apparel needs. We have been embellishing apparel since 1992. We specialize in custom apparel printing and embroidery. Our state-of-the-art equipment and expert staff with over 30 years of collective experience in the screen-printing and embroidery industry allows us to offer the highest quality products and best service anywhere in the United States while still offering the industry’s most competitive prices.
